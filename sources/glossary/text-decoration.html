“text-decoration” is a shortcut for the properties “text-decoration-line”, “text-decoration-style”, and “text-decoration-color”.

Decorate text with one (or more) of the following types of lines:

  • “underline”
  • “overline”
  • “strikethrough”

You can also specify the line style:

  • “solid”
  • “dashed”
  • “double”
  • “dotted”
  • “wavy”

This property also supports color.
